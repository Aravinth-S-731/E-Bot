Let's look how the pre-defined data of the chatbot is processed and a training model is genarated.

JSON file

Tokenizing pre-defined questions

['pay','electricity','bill']

Tokenizing pre-defined tags

['electricity','electricitybill']

Lemmatizing the list of words

*in our case there are
120 lemmatized words.

Lemmatizing the list of tags

*in our case there are
23 lemmatized tags.

The lemmatized words are then processed to bag.Bag of Words (or) BoW is used to calculate the repeated words

*in our case there are
57 bag of words.

texts.pkl

The baged words are then dumped into a pickle file. Pickle(or)'.pkl' is used in serializing and deserializing a Python object structure.

lables.pkl

The lemmatized words in 'texts.pkl' and 'lables.pkl' are now given as input for the keras model. In this keras model the words are interconnected through 3-layers of neuron network. 128 neuron , 64 neuron & number of neuron equal to number of BoW.
After the compilation of lemmatized words, the compiled model is stored as '.h5' file or Hierarchical Data Formats. It is used to store large amount of data in the form of multidimensional arrays. The format is used to store scientific data that is well-organized for quick retrieval and analysis.
